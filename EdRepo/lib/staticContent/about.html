A Default EdRepo Collection

EdRepo (Education Repository) is a simple way for instructors to store collections of course material on the Web and share those materials with others.

View EdRepo's SourceForge page for the latest release, to submit bugs/feature requests, or to read the documentation.

This is the "About" page of a default EdRepo collection. The collection maintainer should replace the contents of this page with information about their collection by logging into an Admin account and editing the "About Page" from the "Information Pages" box on the "Configure This Collection" panel.
